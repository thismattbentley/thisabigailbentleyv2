My name is Abby. I have an amazing husband (who built this website 💕) and two wonderful kiddos, Ezra and Olive. I've been a hairstylist for 5 years now and have loved every minute of it. I'm currently booth renting at Salon Lofts in downtown Matthews, NC.

I always say, a "good" hairstyle is one that fits your lifestyle.
